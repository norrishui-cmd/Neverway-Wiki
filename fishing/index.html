Neverway fishing guide: fish locations, uses, and early money

Fishing guides perform well when they answer location, timing, value, recipe use, and whether a fish should be sold or saved.

Fish location table

After launch, build the table around water area, time of day, weather if relevant, season if relevant, rarity, sell value, recipe use, and gift use. This makes the page useful for both casual players and completionists.

Fishing for early money

If fishing is low-risk and repeatable, it can become a strong early debt strategy. The guide should compare fishing income with crops, gathering, and crafting once verified values exist.
