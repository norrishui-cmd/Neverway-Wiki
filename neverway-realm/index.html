The Neverway Realm Guide: Nightmare Reality, Combat, and Story Role

Understand the nightmare reality called Neverway, how it leaks into Fiona’s world, and how combat, exploration, memory, and the dead god connect to the premise.

Quick answer

Neverway is the nightmare reality that is slowly leaking into Fiona’s world. Searching for it drives the horror story while Fiona also manages farm life, relationships, and combat.

Verified July 18, 2026. This guide separates confirmed facts from unannounced features.

What the name means

Neverway is not simply the title of the island or a generic dungeon label. Official descriptions call it a nightmare reality. Its intrusion creates the contrast between ordinary routines and the game’s psychological and supernatural horror.

Combat and exploration

The game uses fast-paced top-down combat, crafted items, relationship abilities, and traversal tools. The Prologue simulation demonstrates some of these mechanics, but it does not fully establish whether every simulated area is literally part of Neverway.

Story boundary

Fiona becomes the immortal herald of a dead god and is drawn into the search for Neverway. Exact realm rules, bosses, maps, chapter order, and endings remain spoiler-sensitive and require final-build evidence rather than theories.
