The Neverway realm: nightmare reality, combat, and exploration

Official descriptions say a nightmare reality called the Neverway is bleeding into Fiona's life. This page keeps horror, combat, and story coverage separate from cozy island guides.

Why this deserves its own hub

Neverway is not only a farming game. The darker realm changes the search intent: players will look for enemies, bosses, routes, story gates, combat abilities, and how far they can explore before returning to daily life systems.

Spoiler boundaries

Because the Neverway realm is tied to story mystery, this hub should use spoiler-light summaries first, then link to clearly marked boss, ending, and walkthrough pages. That protects casual players while still serving completionist search traffic.
