Editorial Policy

Editorial policy and verification standards

Read the Neverway Guide editorial policy for source handling, verification status, spoiler labeling, launch updates, and correction standards.

Verification policy

Pre-release pages should distinguish confirmed official information from guide planning. Final item values, gift preferences, quest triggers, boss rewards, and ending requirements should be published only after hands-on verification or reliable official confirmation.

Spoiler policy

Beginner and system pages should remain spoiler-light. Story outcomes, ending requirements, late-game boss names, and major choice consequences belong on clearly labeled spoiler pages.

Update policy

Every important guide should be revisited when the prologue changes, a storefront page updates, or the final game launches. Patch-sensitive pages should include version notes once exact data exists.
