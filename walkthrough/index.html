Neverway walkthrough: spoiler-light progression route

A walkthrough page should guide players through the life sim loop without ruining mystery, romance, or late-game discoveries.

Recommended walkthrough structure

Split the walkthrough into clear phases: setup, first week routine, first dangerous route, first major upgrade, relationship unlocks, mid-game exploration, and late-game spoiler content. Keep spoiler-heavy details behind clearly labeled sections.

Daily routing logic

Each route entry should tell players what to do in the morning, what to save inventory space for, who to talk to, when to craft, and when to stop exploring. Link route steps to quests, resources, and combat prep.
