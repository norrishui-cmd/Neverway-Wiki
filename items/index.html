Items

The summary above is supported by the linked official store page, developer update, or Prologue patch record. The page keeps the source context visible because development information, public Prologue behavior, and final-game behavior are not automatically identical.

Corn on the Cob in Neverway: Confirmed Snack Use

Corn on the cob is an officially demonstrated food item that Fiona can equip and eat as a snack at any time. Exact healing, buff, crop growth, recipe, and sale values have not been published.

Instant Noodles in Neverway: Delivery Meal and Time Advancement

Instant noodles are shown as a delivered meal that Fiona can sit down and eat for lunch. Eating the proper meal advances time, unlike using food as an equipped snack.

Berries in Neverway: Aurora Interaction and Special Buff

Official development material connects berries to Aurora: joining her breakfast can improve the relationship and provide a special buff after Fiona eats the berries. The exact buff statistics are not public.

Equipped Snacks in Neverway: Eating Without a Full Meal

Neverway supports equipping food and consuming it as a snack. The official corn example distinguishes this immediate use from sitting down for lunch or dinner, which advances the time block.
