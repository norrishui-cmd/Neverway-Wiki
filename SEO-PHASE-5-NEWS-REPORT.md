# SEO Phase 5: Tab News Expansion

## Published structure

- Added a five-card News module to each primary tab: Guides, Release, Prologue, Platforms, Characters, and FAQ.
- Applied the module to English, Japanese, and Spanish tab pages: 18 updated hub pages.
- Published 30 distinct news topics as independent English URLs.
- Published full Japanese and Spanish counterparts with reciprocal hreflang: 90 new indexable URLs total.
- Increased the sitemap from 162 to 252 indexable canonical URLs.

## Editorial scope

The batch is based on official Neverway sources and covers:

- October 2026 release status and confirmed PC/Switch platforms;
- 500,000+ Steam wishlist milestone and the developer's roughly 20-hour main-story estimate;
- free Prologue availability and Patch 1–4 updates;
- Windows, macOS, Linux, Switch, Steam Deck, PS5, and Xbox status;
- single-player status, save-transfer uncertainty, and six Steam interface/subtitle languages;
- relationship abilities, three daily time blocks, and character routines.

Every article includes a direct answer, dated status, official source, player impact, unconfirmed boundaries, related news, canonical URL, NewsArticle schema, breadcrumbs, and language alternates.

## Validation

- 252 sitemap URLs and 90 news URLs.
- Exactly five news cards on each of 18 localized tab pages.
- Zero broken internal links, duplicate canonical URLs, duplicate titles, invalid JSON-LD, missing hreflang annotations, or sitemap targets.
- AdSense loader and account verification remain present on every generated news page.
