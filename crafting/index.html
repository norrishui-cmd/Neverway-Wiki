Upgrade Path

Neverway crafting guide: recipes, stations, materials, and upgrades

Crafting pages should tell players what to build first, what to save, and which materials unlock the next layer of progress.

Early crafting priorities

Before final values are known, the safest priority order is storage, tool support, healing or food access, and exploration prep. After launch, convert this into a ranked table with material costs and unlock conditions.

Material routes

Each material should eventually have a source page or table row: where it drops, how often it refreshes, whether it is used in gifts, and whether it should be sold. Internal links from material rows to boss, map, and farming pages help SEO depth.
