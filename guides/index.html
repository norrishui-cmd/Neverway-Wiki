Neverway Calendar System: Discovered Events, Birthdays, and Mortgage Deadlines

The calendar records events only after Fiona learns about them. Confirmed examples include mortgage deadlines, birthdays, weekly game nights, and breakfast at Mugs, with character icons also planned for the map and calendar UI.

Neverway Seasons and Year Length: Four 21-Day Seasons

A work-in-progress developer update divides a Neverway year into Spring, Summer, Fall, and Winter, with 21 days in each season. This produces an 84-day design year, although final-build tuning may change.

Neverway Optional Progression: Favor Combat or Farming

Combat and farming are both needed for story progress, but players can emphasize a preferred system. Combat produces loot that can ease farming, while crops and crafted recipes can make combat easier.

Neverway Weekly Routines: Work, Breakfast, Game Nights, and Picnics

Residents have weekly routines that can include jobs, breakfast, oversleeping, game nights, and picnics. The developers reported around 150 routine interactions already implemented during development.

Eating in Neverway: Snacks, Meals, Buffs, and Time

Eating can be immediate through an equipped snack or structured as lunch or dinner. Meals advance time, while food choices can affect buffs, health recovery, and recipes.

LunaOS Computer in Neverway: Confirmed Feature Status

Using a LunaOS computer appears in the original official gameplay feature list. Its applications, upgrades, messaging functions, and relationship with Prologue virtual rendering are not fully explained.
