Neverway Prologue Patch 4 Notes: IJKL, macOS, and Progression Fixes

Neverway Prologue Patch 4 added IJKL rebinding and addressed macOS reporting, resizing, bathtub progression, bench sitting, palette, music, and localization.

Quick answer

Patch 4 was published April 20, 2026. It added IJKL movement rebinding and delivered crash-reporting, progression, visual, audio, and localization changes.

Verified July 18, 2026. This guide separates confirmed facts from unannounced features.

Input and macOS

IJKL keys became supported for movement rebinding. On macOS, crash reporting was improved to investigate submitted crashes; the developers explicitly said this reporting change did not itself resolve those crashes.

Crash and progression

The team possibly addressed a crash caused by resizing the window extremely small with snap resolution. It also possibly fixed Fiona getting stuck after leaving the apartment bathtub and stopped players from sneaking behind Lancelot.

Other corrections

The notes mention an occasional black palette color, Fiona sitting correctly on the simulation bench, music pacing near the fishing area, and Spanish and Russian localization feedback. Steam should install the update before troubleshooting older behavior.
