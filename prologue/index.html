Neverway Prologue Walkthrough Overview and Content Guide

Understand the Neverway Prologue structure, Fiona’s opening, the simulation, Fang, combat training, technical fixes, and its relationship to the full game.

Quick answer

The free Neverway Prologue is a two-part preview: it introduces Fiona’s life before Montgomery Island, then jumps to a disorienting simulation segment that previews combat and exploration.

Verified July 18, 2026. This guide separates confirmed facts from unannounced features.

Fiona’s opening

The opening follows Fiona while work, relationships, and her ability to cope are breaking down. The player completes ordinary tasks, retrieves belongings, makes dialogue choices, and experiences the incident that redirects her life. It is narrative setup, not a shortened farming sandbox.

Dialogue can identify whether Fiona is telling the truth, but the Prologue does not explain every long-term consequence. Avoid treating each response as a permanent romance or ending decision before the full game can be checked.

The simulation

The second section moves into a simulated environment guided by Fang. It introduces sword attacks, dodging, enemies, exploration, and a hook-style traversal tool. Public previews describe it as a look at a later part of Neverway rather than the next ordinary day after the opening.

What the preview proves

Use the Prologue to test story tone, controls, mouse binding, performance, and combat feel. The full game is planned for October 2026 and is estimated at about 20 hours for the main story. Save import is not announced, so treat the preview and final release as separate builds.

Frequently asked questions

Is the Prologue the start of the final game?

It includes Fiona’s opening but also jumps to a later simulation segment.

Does it include the full farm loop?

No.

Who guides Fiona in the simulation?

Fang.
