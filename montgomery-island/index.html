Montgomery Island guide: Sellmart, friends, routines, and exploration

Official Neverway materials place Fiona's new life on Montgomery Island. This page turns that confirmed setting into a hub for shops, NPCs, routines, and map routes.

What Montgomery Island means for the guide

Montgomery Island is the safe-side anchor for Neverway's life sim systems: meeting characters, managing daily routines, building a new life, and preparing for riskier exploration. A good guide should separate island-life pages from spoiler-heavy Neverway realm pages.

Sellmart and economy pages

Official screenshots and page text reference Sellmart as part of the island loop. After launch, Sellmart should become a dedicated shop table with stock, prices, refresh timing, and which purchases matter for farming, crafting, gifts, and mortgage progress.

Friends, schedules, and routines

The official site mentions over ten charming characters with their own routines and storylines. This creates a clear need for schedule pages, birthday pages, gift pages, and spoiler-light relationship routing.
