Neverway Systems

This page records a named character, item, activity, system, feature, Demo element, or location supported by official material. It does not fill missing values with genre assumptions.

Systems

The summary above is supported by the linked official store page, developer update, or Prologue patch record. The page keeps the source context visible because development information, public Prologue behavior, and final-game behavior are not automatically identical.

Neverway Calendar System: Discovered Events, Birthdays, and Mortgage Deadlines

The calendar records events only after Fiona learns about them. Confirmed examples include mortgage deadlines, birthdays, weekly game nights, and breakfast at Mugs, with character icons also planned for the map and calendar UI.

Neverway Seasons and Year Length: Four 21-Day Seasons

A work-in-progress developer update divides a Neverway year into Spring, Summer, Fall, and Winter, with 21 days in each season. This produces an 84-day design year, although final-build tuning may change.

Neverway Optional Progression: Favor Combat or Farming

Combat and farming are both needed for story progress, but players can emphasize a preferred system. Combat produces loot that can ease farming, while crops and crafted recipes can make combat easier.

Neverway Food and Time System: Snacks Versus Meals

Food has at least two confirmed uses: equip it for a snack, or cook and sit down for lunch or dinner. A proper meal advances the current time block and can interact with buffs, health recovery, and recipe learning.

Neverway Weekly Routines: Work, Breakfast, Game Nights, and Picnics

Residents have weekly routines that can include jobs, breakfast, oversleeping, game nights, and picnics. The developers reported around 150 routine interactions already implemented during development.

Neverway Activity Effects: Buffs, Health Recovery, and Recipe Learning

Activities that advance time can also add or remove buffs, recover health, or teach new recipes. The exact outcome depends on the activity; a complete numerical table is not yet public.

What is not confirmed

Only the named behavior and facts in the summary are confirmed. Exact numerical values, hidden triggers, final prices, complete schedules, romance requirements, drop rates, rewards, and final-build balance remain outside the index until an official source or direct game evidence supplies a specific answer.
