Neverway Prologue Simulation: Fang, Combat, and Story Context

Understand the Neverway Prologue simulation, why it follows Fiona’s opening, what mechanics it teaches, and which story questions remain unanswered.

Quick answer

The simulation is the Prologue’s second major section. It jumps from Fiona’s opening story to an unstable environment where Fang guides combat and exploration training.

Verified July 18, 2026. This guide separates confirmed facts from unannounced features.

Abrupt transition

The Prologue is designed to show both Neverway’s emotional narrative and action RPG side. The simulation does not behave like the next ordinary day after the opening; it exposes a different point in Fiona’s experience without complete context.

Mechanics

Fiona uses a sword, dodge movement, and hook-like traversal device while encountering enemies and environmental obstacles. Patch notes also reference a simulation bench and corrected her sitting animation there.

Interpretation boundary

Fang’s identity, the simulation’s operator, and its exact story position are not fully disclosed. Document visible instructions, environments, glitches, and mechanics while leaving theories labeled until the full game resolves them.
