Neverway World Guide: Montgomery Island, Simulation, and Nightmare Realm

Explore the confirmed Neverway settings, including Fiona’s city opening, Montgomery Island, the Prologue simulation, and the nightmare reality leaking into her world.

Quick answer

Neverway moves between Fiona’s grounded life, her new farm on Montgomery Island, a mysterious simulation, and the nightmare reality called Neverway that is slowly leaking into the ordinary world.

Verified July 18, 2026. This guide separates confirmed facts from unannounced features.

Fiona’s city opening

The Prologue begins with Fiona’s work and personal life collapsing. Apartments, streets, public transport, and her former workplace establish a grounded emotional setting before horror distorts the familiar environment.

Montgomery Island

The full life-sim structure takes place around Fiona’s remote island home. Official materials show farming, fishing, Sellmart, homestead decoration, friendships, routines, meals, and mortgage pressure. Exact shop inventories and map routes wait for final-game verification.

Simulation and Neverway

The Prologue jumps to a simulation where Fang guides Fiona through combat and traversal. Separately, Neverway is described as a nightmare reality leaking into her world. Their full relationship remains a story mystery, so location pages separate visible evidence from interpretation.
