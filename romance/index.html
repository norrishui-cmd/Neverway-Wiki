Neverway romance guide: routes, gifts, and heart events

A dedicated romance URL targets one of the highest-value life sim search intents: who can you date, what gifts work, and how events unlock.

How romance coverage should work

Until final data is verified, the page should explain the expected categories: romance candidates, relationship points, gifts, dates, event triggers, and possible endings. After launch, each candidate should link to a dedicated profile page.

Gift and event loop

The ideal romance guide connects gift preferences to event unlocks. Players should be able to answer three questions fast: what should I give, when can I give it, and what happens at the next relationship threshold?

Spoiler handling

Western guide users often want spoiler-light romance advice. Use summary tables for safe information and separate clearly marked sections for late-game events, endings, or major story consequences.
