Who Is Fang in Neverway? Prologue Simulation Role Explained

Fang is the mysterious guide in Neverway Prologue’s simulation, introducing Fiona to combat and exploration while the full relationship remains unexplained.

Quick answer

Fang guides Fiona inside the Prologue simulation. The preview establishes that role but intentionally withholds the full context of who Fang is and why the simulation exists.

Verified July 18, 2026. This guide separates confirmed facts from unannounced features.

Visible role

After the grounded opening, the Prologue jumps to a simulated section. Fang guides Fiona while she learns to fight, dodge, explore, and use a hook-style traversal tool. It previews later mechanics rather than explaining every connection.

The mystery

The Prologue does not fully define Fang’s relationship to Fiona, the simulation’s origin, or how the sequence fits the October release. Community theories are discussion, not confirmed biography or lore.

Reading the sequence

Public previews connect the unstable environment to Fiona’s memories. Focus on explicit labels, Fang’s instructions, and visible glitches. Later pages should separate confirmed dialogue from interpretation and mark story spoilers.
