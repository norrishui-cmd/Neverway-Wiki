Fiona in Neverway: Protagonist, Prologue Story, and Gameplay Role

Learn who Fiona is in Neverway, why she leaves her old life, how the Prologue introduces her, and how farming, relationships, choices, and combat shape her story.

Quick answer

Fiona is Neverway’s fixed playable protagonist. After her city life collapses, she starts over on a farm and becomes the immortal herald of a dead god.

Verified July 18, 2026. This guide separates confirmed facts from unannounced features.

Prologue setup

The Prologue presents Fiona at a low point involving work, isolation, and strained relationships. Ordinary tasks and dialogue choices communicate her mental state before a disturbing incident redirects her life.

Island life

The full game connects Fiona’s farm, mortgage, meals, crafting, fishing, decorating, and relationships to a larger story. She can meet or date characters, and those bonds grant buffs or combat abilities.

Neverway

Fiona becomes the immortal herald of a dead god while the nightmare reality called Neverway leaks into her world. The simulation previews her sword, dodge, and traversal tools. No alternate protagonist or character creator is announced.

Frequently asked questions

Can you create a protagonist?

No; Fiona is fixed.

Is Fiona playable in the Prologue?

Yes.
