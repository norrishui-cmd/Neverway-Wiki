Neverway Characters: Fiona, Fang, Relationships, and Routines

Meet confirmed Neverway characters, starting with protagonist Fiona and simulation guide Fang, and understand routines, relationships, buffs, and romance.

Quick answer

Fiona is Neverway’s fixed protagonist. Fang guides her in the Prologue simulation. The full game promises more than 10 characters with routines and storylines who can be befriended or dated.

Verified July 18, 2026. This guide separates confirmed facts from unannounced features.

Fiona

Fiona leaves a collapsing city life and begins again on a farm before becoming the immortal herald of a dead god. She is the only playable protagonist announced; there is no character creator. Her choices, relationships, daily routine, and combat abilities shape the journey.

Fang

Fang appears as Fiona’s guide inside the simulated part of the Prologue. The sequence teaches combat and exploration while withholding their connection and the simulation’s purpose. Visible actions can be documented, but fan theories should not be presented as story canon.

Relationships and routines

Official descriptions promise 10+ characters with unique routines and stories. Characters work, eat breakfast, oversleep, attend game nights, or share picnics. Bonds unlock buffs and combat abilities, and the game contains information showing whether someone is dateable.

Aurora in Neverway: Scientist, Bookshop, Breakfast, and Berry Buff

Aurora is a scientist who studies bizarre events through facts, helps Fiona study manuals or find books at her bookshop, and eats breakfast outside her home. Sitting with her can strengthen the relationship and grant a special buff after eating berries.

Lancelot in Neverway Prologue: Confirmed Interactions and Patch Changes

Lancelot is a named Prologue character connected to two official fixes: Patch 3 stopped a virtual-rendering visit exploit, and Patch 4 prevented players from sneaking behind him. His full-game role and romance status remain unconfirmed.
