Confirmed Mechanics

Neverway Combat Guide: Sword, Dodge, Abilities, and Grappling Hook

Learn Neverway’s confirmed combat structure: top-down sword attacks, dodge movement, crafted items, relationship abilities, and grappling-hook traversal.

Quick answer

Neverway uses fast-paced top-down action combat. The Prologue simulation demonstrates sword attacks, dodging, enemies, and a hook-style tool for crossing gaps.

Verified July 18, 2026. This guide separates confirmed facts from unannounced features.

Basic loop

Fiona fights in real time rather than through turn-based menus. The Prologue teaches a direct attack and dodge-focused loop, so spacing, recovery, and recognizing enemy movement matter more than farm statistics during these sequences.

Build customization

The official Steam description says crafted items customize playstyle, while friendship bonds unlock buffs and combat abilities. Exact damage, cooldowns, optimal builds, and a full weapon list require final-game testing and are not inferred from trailers.

Traversal

Public previews show a grappling-hook-style tool crossing gaps during the simulation. It supports exploration and player expression rather than proving a complete equipment tier. Combat connects to the nightmare reality that is leaking into Fiona’s world.
