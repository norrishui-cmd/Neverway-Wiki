Farm Systems

Neverway farming guide: crops, profit, seasons, and daily routine

Farming should be explained as part of the wider survival loop: money, food, crafting materials, gifts, and debt progress.

Daily farm routine

A strong beginner routine starts with crops and basic gathering before risky exploration. Once final stamina rules are known, this page should show a morning route, tool priority, and when to stop farming for social or combat progress.

Crop profit framework

Post-launch crop tables should list seed cost, growth time, regrowth, sell value, profit per day, cooking use, and gift value. Profit alone is not enough if a crop is needed for a quest, recipe, or relationship route.
