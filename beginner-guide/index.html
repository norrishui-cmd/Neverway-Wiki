Starter Route

Neverway beginner guide: first day and first week priorities

Start with a stable routine before optimizing. This page turns Neverway's farming, crafting, combat, and relationship systems into a simple early-game route.

First day goals

Your first day should be about learning the map, checking available tools, gathering basic materials, and identifying which actions cost time or stamina. Do not rush deep exploration until you know how healing, food, and return routes work.

  • Inspect the farm and storage.
  • Gather safe nearby resources.
  • Meet key NPCs for future gift and event tracking.
  • Mark any locked areas or suspicious paths for later.

First week priorities

The best first week structure is a loop: grow a basic income source, craft one useful station, test combat carefully, and keep relationship progress moving. A guide page should avoid over-optimization before final numbers are known, but the routine itself is stable.

Common early mistakes

New players are likely to waste rare materials, ignore food, overextend at night, or gift items before understanding NPC preferences. After launch, this page should link directly to the gift guide, crafting guide, and combat guide.
