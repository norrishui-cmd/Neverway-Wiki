Neverway Platforms: PC, Nintendo Switch, PS5, and Xbox Status

Neverway is confirmed for PC and Nintendo Switch in October 2026. Check Windows, Mac, Linux, PS5, Xbox, Steam Deck, and multiplayer status.

Quick answer

Neverway is officially planned for PC and Nintendo Switch in October 2026. Steam lists Windows, macOS, and SteamOS + Linux. PS5 and Xbox versions have not been announced.

Verified July 18, 2026. This guide separates confirmed facts from unannounced features.

Confirmed launch platforms

The official Neverway site states “PC and Switch.” The Steam listing provides Windows, macOS, and SteamOS + Linux tabs, while Nintendo has published a Switch release trailer. No exact October day is listed yet.

Features that are confirmed

Steam lists single-player, Steam Achievements, Steam Cloud, and Family Sharing. The store page does not list online co-op, local co-op, or multiplayer. Language and feature availability can differ on Switch, so wait for the final eShop listing before assuming parity.

Is Neverway on PC?

Yes. PC is confirmed, and Steam lists Windows, macOS, and SteamOS + Linux.

Nintendo Switch is confirmed. PlayStation and Xbox are not announced.
