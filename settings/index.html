Neverway settings guide: controls, accessibility, and display options

Settings pages are practical SEO pages because players search for controller support, readability, audio balance, and platform-specific setup details.

Controls and controller support

After launch, document keyboard controls, controller prompts, rebinding support, and any platform differences. For a life sim RPG, quick access to tools, inventory, and map controls matters more than raw action speed.

Readability and accessibility

Track text size, contrast, UI scaling, subtitle behavior, screen shake, flashing effects, and audio sliders. These details help both PC and handheld players decide which platform fits them.
