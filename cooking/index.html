Recipe Guide

Neverway cooking guide: recipes, healing, gifts, and exploration prep

Cooking connects farming, fishing, combat, and romance. A strong recipe page tells players what to eat, sell, save, or gift.

Recipe table structure

Each recipe should include ingredients, unlock source, healing value, stamina value, sell value, gift relevance, and whether it is useful before combat or long exploration routes.

Best meals for exploration

After launch, rank meals by recovery per ingredient, ease of farming, and inventory efficiency. Link rare ingredients back to farming, fishing, and map pages.
