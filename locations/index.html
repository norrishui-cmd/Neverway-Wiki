Mugs in Neverway: Breakfast Event and Calendar Discovery

Mugs is a named location associated with a breakfast event. The calendar can add that breakfast after Fiona visits on the relevant day or learns about it from another character.

Aurora’s Bookshop in Neverway: Books and Manual Study

Aurora is connected to a bookshop where she can help Fiona find books or study new manuals. The shop name, inventory, prices, hours, and exact map position have not been published.

Aurora’s House Breakfast Spot in Neverway

Aurora eats breakfast outside her house in the morning, and Fiona can sit beside her. The interaction can deepen their relationship and connect to a special berry buff.
